Faculty of Commerce

The Faculty of Commerce is dedicated to equipping students with the knowledge and skills necessary for a successful career in the business world. Our comprehensive programs cover key areas such as accounting, finance, management, and economics. We emphasize practical application and critical analysis, ensuring that our graduates are well-prepared to meet the challenges of a dynamic global economy.

Students benefit from a curriculum that combines theoretical foundations with real-world case studies and internships, preparing them for roles in corporate management, financial services, entrepreneurship, and more.
