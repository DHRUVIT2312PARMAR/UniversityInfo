Faculty of Science

The Faculty of Science is a vibrant hub of discovery and innovation, offering a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs. We are dedicated to providing students with a deep understanding of scientific principles through hands-on research, advanced laboratory work, and engaging lectures. Our faculty members are leading experts in their fields, and our curriculum is designed to prepare students for successful careers in research, technology, medicine, and more.

Key disciplines include Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology, and Computer Science.
